Personality OS Dashboard

Track your growth using psychological frameworks and personal insights.

Overview

This dashboard uses models like the Iceberg Model, Big Five Traits, Self‑Determination Theory (SDT) and PERMA to guide self‑development. Use the sections below to reflect on your behaviours and beliefs.

Framework Summaries

Iceberg Model

Behaviour is just the visible tip; beliefs, emotions and experiences lie beneath the surface ([www.munich-business-school.de](https://www.munich-business-school.de/en/l/business-studies-dictionary/iceberg-model)). Reflect on hidden drivers behind your actions.

Big Five Traits (OCEAN)

Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness and Neuroticism describe personality dimensions ([www.verywellmind.com](https://www.verywellmind.com/the-big-five-personality-dimensions-2795422)). Consider your strengths and areas for growth.

Self‑Determination Theory

Meeting the needs for autonomy, competence and relatedness fuels motivation and well‑being ([www.verywellmind.com](https://www.verywellmind.com/what-is-self-determination-theory-2795387)).

PERMA

Positive emotion, Engagement, Relationships, Meaning and Accomplishment are pillars of flourishing. Track activities that support each pillar.

Growth Log & Notes

Add notes about daily wins, challenges and reflections. Tag them to frameworks or traits.
